About

SEA GC Realty.

A South Florida boutique real estate brokerage a Florida limited liability company. We serve buyers, sellers, investors, and the agents who serve them across Palm Beach and Broward Counties.

Why it exists.

Most South Florida brokerages are one of two things: a giant national franchise that treats every agent as a number and every client as a lead score, or a one-broker shop that does deals on the side. We are neither. SEA GC Realty is a small, broker-owned brokerage with a focused service area, a real lead pipeline, and a fee structure designed to attract agents who would otherwise be at Florida's Best Realty — license hangers, part-timers, and serious producers who do not want to subsidize someone else's overhead.

Most of our clients come to us because someone in their family or business circle worked with the broker on a previous transaction. We like it that way. The growth comes from doing good work for the people we already serve, not from billboard advertising.

The broker.

Ben Shoshan
Broker of Record
FL Real Estate License No. BK3471395

Ben Shoshan is a Florida-licensed real estate broker, an active foreclosure investor across Palm Beach and Broward Counties, and a law student at St. Thomas University School of Law. He founded SEA GC LLC in 2023 as a holding company for his construction, development, and real estate operations. SEA GC Realty is the brokerage arm.

Day to day he closes deals, runs the foreclosure scout pipeline, supervises associated agents under his broker license, and supports the firm's investor clients with the same analytics he uses on his own acquisitions. He is not a part-time broker who took the test and never used it.

He has built proprietary internal systems for investor sourcing and analytics.
