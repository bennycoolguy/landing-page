Careers

A brokerage built for agents who want to keep their commission.

No desk fees. No monthly minimums. No tier-padding tricks. A flat $350 per transaction, a real lead pipeline, and a broker who actually picks up the phone.

How you get paid.

Tier 1
70 / 30

Self-generated deals, your first three closings. 70% to you, 30% to the brokerage. Same on every transaction until you cross the threshold.

Tier 2
80 / 20

After your third closing, the split bumps automatically. 80% to you, 20% to the brokerage. Permanent. No annual reset.

Broker-supplied leads
50 / 50

When the brokerage feeds you the lead, the split is 50/50 regardless of tier. The leads are good — expired BeachesMLS listings pulled and scored weekly — so the split reflects that.

$350 transaction fee.

Flat. Per closing. Covers brokerage overhead, MLS, transaction coordination, escrow administration, and supervision. That is the only fee you pay. No monthly desk fee, no E&O fee separate, no marketing fee, no "tech fee," no extras at year-end.

No monthly minimums.

If you want to hang your license and close one or two deals a year, fine. If you want to grind and close two a month, fine. We are not going to charge you a monthly retainer either way. The fee scales with revenue, not seat time.

The Leads

Real pipeline, not "join our CRM."

Every week, the brokerage pulls expired, cancelled, and withdrawn listings from BeachesMLS, scores them on conversion likelihood (recency, DOM at expiration, price reductions, list price), and distributes the top leads to associated agents. The data feed comes from our licensed MLS data access — not from a third-party scraper, and not from PropStream.

PBC + Broward

Two counties, all single-family, condo, and townhouse residential.

Weekly refresh

Fresh expired and cancelled listings every Sunday night.

Scored, not dumped

Top leads by conversion likelihood, so you work the best ones first.

This works well if you are…

  • ✓ A new agent who wants a real broker, real leads, and a fair split — not a 100-agent megabrokerage that ignores you
  • ✓ An experienced agent ready to keep 80% of your commission with a flat fee on the rest
  • ✓ A license-hanger who closes one or two deals a year and does not want a monthly nut
  • ✓ A part-time agent with a day job who needs flexibility

This is probably not for you if…

  • ✕ You want a corner office, an assistant, and a printer in the lobby
  • ✕ You expect the brokerage to do the call-outs and texts for you
  • ✕ You need someone to walk every showing with you for the first six months
  • ✕ You want a brand name that gets you appointments before they meet you
